Philippines

General Santos City, abbreviated as GenSan, is a city located on the southernmost island of Mindanao in the Philippines. GenSan is the only urbanized center of the Soccsksargen Province, an administrative region located at the south-central of Mindanao designated as Region XII. The city is highly urbanized, being the only shopping capital of the Region XII. The city can be reached by land, sea and air, with its airport capable of handling international flights and wide-bodied aircrafts, and a port capable of handling international shipping. Although a highly urbanized, its two major industry are in agriculture and fishing.
